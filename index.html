What is it?

  • Syntax of OCaml
  • Transforms to OCaml and compiles to JS
  • Also compiles to assembly

What can it do for us?

  • Static, inferred types
  • Functional purity and immutability, tail-call optimization...
  • ...but with escape hatches for easy side effects, mutations, etc.
  • Fast builds and small output sizes
  • Carry on using Yarn, Webpack, all your favourite node modules etc.

What about other compile to JS languages?

  • Elm
  • ClojureScript
  • PureScript
  • CoffeeScript
